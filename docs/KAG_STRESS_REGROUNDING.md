# KAG STRESS REGROUNDING

## Goal

Teach the derived substrate to become more honest under drift.

When a projection, pack, or bridge becomes stale, incomplete, or contradictory, `aoa-kag` should narrow consumer posture and route back toward source-owned meaning.

## Why `aoa-kag` owns this

`aoa-kag` owns derived substrate structure, provenance-aware lift packs, normalized projections, retrieval-ready packs, and return-regrounding seams.

Stress posture belongs here because it changes:

- whether a derived surface remains consumable
- whether consumers should prefer source-first routes
- whether a projection should be quarantined
- what regrounding work is required before the surface becomes trusted again

It does not move authored meaning out of source repos.

## Additive shape

Prefer additive derived-layer objects such as:

- `schemas/projection_health_receipt_v1.json`
- `schemas/regrounding_ticket_v1.json`
- matching examples

These can sit beside existing `generated/*` and `manifests/*` families without changing source ownership.

## Health states

Wave 3 uses a small health vocabulary:

- `healthy`
- `degraded_source_first`
- `quarantined`
- `regrounding`

The important thing is not the exact words.
The important thing is that consumer posture narrows when the derived surface becomes untrustworthy.

## Inputs worth trusting

Prefer these triggers, in order:

1. source-owned receipts that show donor drift or missing dependencies
2. KAG validator failures or explicit generation mismatches
3. reviewed cross-repo evidence that names the affected projection
4. optional routing or memo context only as secondary shaping signals

## Consumer posture

A stressed KAG surface should publish a narrower consumer posture such as:

- `source_first`
- `route_pack_only`
- `do_not_expand`

That posture is about safe use of the derived surface.
It is not a new claim about source meaning.

## Guardrails

- do not silently regenerate and republish drifted surfaces as if nothing happened
- do not let KAG overrule source-owned truth
- do not use quarantine as a permanent hiding place without review
- do not widen consumer posture while the projection is degraded
- do not erase provenance when opening a regrounding ticket

## Acceptance shape

A healthy wave-3 landing would make it possible to point to:

- one projection-health receipt family
- one regrounding-ticket family
- one documented path from degraded or quarantined state back to validated use
- one explicit source-first fallback route
