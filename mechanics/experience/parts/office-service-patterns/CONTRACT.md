# Office Service Pattern Contract

This part may define bounded KAG candidate shapes for office/service patterns
that have passed upstream owner and operator gates.

Allowed payload:

- multi-office train precedent candidates;
- office service pattern candidates;
- owner repo refs, evidence refs, train refs, forced-adoption stop-lines, and
  candidate status.

Stop-lines:

- no live office activation;
- no assistant self-authority;
- no release approval;
- no runtime dispatch;
- no proof certification;
- no forced adoption.
