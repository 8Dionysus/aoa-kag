# Experience Parts

`mechanics/experience/parts/` holds active KAG-side Experience sub-mechanics.

Use a part when an experience-derived payload family has a local owner split,
contract, focused validation, and artifacts clearer inside the part than in
root KAG districts.

## Active parts

| Part | Role | Public contract companions |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `governance-precedent` | Governance precedent candidate, promotion dossier, and ToS bridge contracts. | part-local `schemas/`, `examples/`, `docs/`, and `tests/` |
| `office-service-patterns` | Office train and service pattern candidate contracts. | part-local `schemas/`, `examples/`, `docs/`, and `tests/` |
| `release-patterns` | Release, installation, and post-release pattern candidate contracts. | part-local `schemas/`, `examples/`, `docs/`, and `tests/` |

Root schemas and examples should not be used for active Experience part
contracts once the owning part is active.
