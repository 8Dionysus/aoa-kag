# Governance Precedent Contract

This part may define bounded KAG candidate shapes for reviewed governance
precedents.

Allowed payload:

- policy precedent node candidates;
- reviewed governance promotion dossiers;
- ToS dossier bridge records that keep direct writes forbidden;
- evidence, decision, authority, retention, and status fields.

Stop-lines:

- no governance execution;
- no council or veto authority;
- no forced owner adoption;
- no proof certification;
- no runtime mutation;
- no direct Tree-of-Sophia canon write.
