Experience governance pressure.

- governance precedent harvest route docs;
- policy precedent candidate schemas and examples;
- governance promotion and ToS dossier bridge contracts;
- focused schema/example contract tests.

certification, runtime state, owner adoption, or direct Tree-of-Sophia writes.
