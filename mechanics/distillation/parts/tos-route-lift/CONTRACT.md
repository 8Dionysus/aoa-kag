# ToS Route Lift Contract

This part owns the operation contract for AOA-K-0010.

It requires:

- route inputs to remain canonical Tree of Sophia refs;
- node family counts and order to stay stable;
- authority refs to stay canonical `Tree-of-Sophia/ToS/canon/**/node.json`;
- relation edges to resolve inside the projected route node set.
- raw-table intake posture to stay part-local and explicitly non-activated
  until a separate source, manifest, schema, generated pack, and validation
  route exist.

It forbids:

- intake refs;
- literal-id residue;
- canon authorship;
- routing ownership;
- graph normalization.
