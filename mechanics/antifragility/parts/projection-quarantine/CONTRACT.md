# Projection Quarantine Contract

This part may define how KAG marks a derived surface unsafe for expansion.

Allowed payload:

- quarantine docs;
- projection-health schema expectations for `quarantined`,
  `source_first`, and `do_not_expand` posture.

Stop-lines:

- no deletion of provenance;
- no source-owned meaning rewrite;
- no healthy posture while evidence is unresolved;
- no hidden or permanent quarantine without review.
