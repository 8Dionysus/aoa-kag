# Retrieval Outage Regrounding Contract

This part may define bounded KAG regrounding tickets for retrieval outage and
degraded projection return.

Allowed payload:

- projection refs;
- trigger receipt refs;
- donor rebuild plans;
- validation steps;
- expected outputs;
- reviewer posture and status.

Stop-lines:

- no runtime repair authority;
- no playbook re-entry authority;
- no proof verdict;
- no direct source mutation;
- no synthetic continuity.
